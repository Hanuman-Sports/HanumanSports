Shipping & Returns Policy

At Hanuman Sports, we strive to make your shopping experience seamless. Learn about our shipping options and hassle-free return policy.

Shipping Information

Domestic Shipping

We offer fast and reliable shipping across India. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment verification.

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days ₹99 (Free on orders above ₹2999)
Express Shipping 2-3 business days ₹199
Next Day Delivery* 1 business day ₹399

*Next day delivery available only in select metropolitan areas. Order must be placed before 12 PM IST.

Important Note

Delivery times are estimates and may be affected by factors beyond our control such as weather conditions, customs delays, or carrier delays. During festive seasons, delivery times may be extended.

International Shipping

We now ship to select international destinations. International orders typically take 10-20 business days for delivery depending on the destination.

International shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the weight of your order and destination. Customs duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient.

Customs & Import Taxes

International customers are responsible for all customs and import taxes that may be applied by their country. Hanuman Sports is not responsible for these additional charges.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with your tracking number and a link to track your package.

If you have any questions about your order's status, please contact our customer service team with your order number.

| Return & Exchange Policy

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a 30-day return policy for most items.

Eligibility Conditions:

  • Item must be unused and in its original condition
  • Original packaging and tags must be intact
  • Return request must be made within 30 days of delivery
  • Proof of purchase is required

Non-Returnable Items:

  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Underwear and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Products without original packaging
  • Products damaged due to misuse

How to Return

Follow these simple steps to return an item:

  1. Contact our customer service team to initiate the return process
  2. Package the item securely in its original packaging
  3. Include the original invoice and reason for return
  4. Ship the package to our return address

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund or exchange within 5-7 business days.

Refund Processing

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Depending on your bank, it may take 7-10 business days for the refund to reflect in your account.

Warranty Claims

Most of our products come with manufacturer warranties. If you experience any issues with your product during the warranty period, please contact us with:

  • Your order details
  • Photos/videos of the issue
  • Description of the problem

We will guide you through the warranty claim process with the manufacturer.

| Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to receive my order?

Delivery times depend on your location and the shipping method chosen. Typically, domestic orders arrive within 5-7 business days with standard shipping. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer free standard shipping on all orders over ₹2999 within India. For orders below this amount, a flat shipping fee of ₹99 applies.

What if I receive a damaged product?

If you receive a damaged product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged item and packaging. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no extra cost.

Can I change my delivery address after ordering?

We can only change the delivery address if the order hasn't been shipped yet. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible with your order number and the updated address.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.
